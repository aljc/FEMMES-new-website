2015 UChicago FEMMES Code Camp

UChicago FEMMES is proud to launch its first annual Capstone event: the UChicago FEMMES Code Camp!

In partnership with the Office of Special Programs, we will bring 200 middle school girls to campus on Saturday, February 28th for a full day of computer science workshops.

The event will take place at the University of Chicago, and will feature a keynote by Jenna Suarez, the Chicago Regional Program Manager of Code.org, and activities in computer science led by undergraduate students. The event will run from 10AM to 5PM. Lunch and snacks will be provided. Our goal is to have the girls go home excited and enthusiastic to continue learning about computer science.

Registration for the event is now closed. However, you can help us by volunteering for the Code Camp! To sign up to volunteer for the event, please fill out this form.
